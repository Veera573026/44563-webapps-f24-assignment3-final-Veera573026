Violet Phoenix - The Vigilant

Description:

Violet Phoenix the Vigilant rises from the ashes of destruction to bring justice and protection to the world. With wings that shimmer in violet light, she watches over the city, ready to strike down evil with swift, fiery justice. Her vigilant nature ensures that no threat goes unnoticed, and no enemy escapes her fiery gaze.

Powers:

  • Rebirth from ashes, making her nearly immortal.
  • Ability to control and manipulate fire.
  • Superhuman vigilance, allowing her to see through deception and detect danger from great distances.
