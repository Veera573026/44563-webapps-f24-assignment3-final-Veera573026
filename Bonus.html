Phastos

Description:

Phastos is an Eternal, a nearly immortal race created thousands of years ago by the alien Celestials. Being a weapon-smith, Phastos was mistaken for the Olympian god Hephaestus during the days of ancient Greece.

Powers:

  • Super agility and speed.
  • Expert swordsmanship.
  • Unmatched strength in battle.
